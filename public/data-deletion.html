Data Deletion Request

If you want to request deletion of your data collected by our app (e.g., messages, reminders, or goals), please follow the instructions below:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request. Include your WhatsApp number (e.g. +94771234567) used with the app.

Option 2: In-App Message

You can also send the message "Delete my data" to the WhatsApp assistant and we will process the request.

All deletion requests will be processed within 7 days.
